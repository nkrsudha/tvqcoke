Our Company

Our History

TVQ Coke Oven Consultants Founder Mr J.Rajaraman and group of experienced coke oven professionals, engineers having rich experience and proven track records in Coke oven industry. Mr J.Rajaraman is qualified Engineer and Management professional having 25 years of industrial experience in the field of Refractory and Metallurgical Coke in India. He and his team has designed, renovated and operated more than 1.75 Million TPA installed capacity of Non-recovery / Heat recovery Metallurgical Coke plant in India (Gujarat, Karnataka, West Bengal and Odisha). He has designed, build and operated 0.57 Million TPA green field Coke Oven Plant with centralised Coal handling, Stamping cum Pushing Machine, Modified Wet Quench Tower, Centralised Coke handling, Laboratory, storage sheds and rain harvesting.

In the last 25 years TVQ Founder has proved himself as a hardworking and result oriented leader in the field Coke oven industry and has grown over the years to have such leading position like GM-GNRE & SR.VP – ECL and will be continuing this progressive journey and new emerging opportunities. We at TVQ are committed to coke making solution for “Make in India” dream to reduce over dependency on Imports.

Vision

  • To provide integrated services for Coke Oven project and operation with International standards through Value and Quality
  • To establish our company as trusted solution provider for Metallurgical Coke making in India.

Mission

  • To provide inexpensive, innovative and Modern coke making facilities in India
  • To reduce over dependency on imports and increase domestic coke making capacity
  • To produce inexpensive coke making in India with finest quality through highest operating procedure and Research & development
